# Protection Plans — visual brief per archetype

Cards are 1080×1080. Dark slate gradient surface (matches the live
app). Each card has its own accent color reflecting the cultural
register of the plan. Pull-quote philosophy line + allocation row
visualizing where money goes.

## Shared composition

Every card has the same structural elements:
- **Kicker chip** (top-left): `PROTECTION PLAN · {NAME}` in the
  archetype accent color.
- **Hero element**: archetype-specific (motif, geometric pattern,
  or symbolic shape) — see per-archetype briefs below.
- **Philosophy line**: a single sentence capturing the cultural
  ethos. Max 2 lines on the card.
- **Allocation row**: 3–5 small pills showing the asset mix
  (e.g., "cUSD · cEUR · KESm" for Africapitalism).
- **Footer**: TrustMark (archetype accent) + DIVERSIFI wordmark +
  date / "Verifiable on-chain" affordance.

## The 7 archetypes

### 1. Africapitalism
- **Accent:** Warm terracotta / ochre (#d97706 amber-600 or
  #dc6803). Pulls from East African ceramic / Yoruba textiles.
- **Hero motif:** Adinkra-inspired geometric pattern (faint, NOT
  literal Adinkra symbols — abstract geometry only). Or a stylized
  continental outline.
- **Philosophy line:** "Keep wealth in African economies. Every
  cUSD, cEUR, KESm stays close to home."
- **Allocation pills:** cUSD · cEUR · KESm · COPm
- **TrustMark:** Terracotta circle.

### 2. Buen Vivir
- **Accent:** Andean teal / jade (#0d9488 teal-600). Pulls from
  Andean textiles, jungle/highland greens.
- **Hero motif:** Stepped pyramid pattern (suggested chakana cross)
  or a horizon-line landscape.
- **Philosophy line:** "Balance material wealth with community and
  the natural world."
- **Allocation pills:** COPm · BRZ · USDC · Community fund
- **TrustMark:** Teal circle.

### 3. Confucian
- **Accent:** Deep ink red / vermilion (#b91c1c red-700). Pulls
  from East Asian seals, lacquerware.
- **Hero motif:** A single calligraphic-feel mark, or stacked
  horizontal bars suggesting hexagram patterns (I Ching reference —
  abstract, not literal).
- **Philosophy line:** "Long-term stability. Low volatility.
  Patience as a strategy."
- **Allocation pills:** USDC · cUSD · RWA Treasuries · Bonds
- **TrustMark:** Vermilion circle.

### 4. Gotong Royong
- **Accent:** Sunset orange / batik (#ea580c orange-600). Pulls
  from Southeast Asian batik, Indonesian sunsets.
- **Hero motif:** Interlocking ring pattern (mutual aid network
  visualized) or a stylized circle of hands.
- **Philosophy line:** "Community-first. Shared risk. We rise
  together."
- **Allocation pills:** cUSD · IDRT · PHPT · Community pool
- **TrustMark:** Sunset orange circle.

### 5. Islamic Finance
- **Accent:** Emerald (#059669 emerald-600). Pulls from Islamic
  geometric art, mosque architecture.
- **Hero motif:** 8-point star or interlocking geometric pattern
  (abstract, respectful — no literal religious imagery).
- **Philosophy line:** "Sharia-compliant. No interest-bearing
  assets. Ethical by design."
- **Allocation pills:** USDC (Sharia-screened) · Sukuk · Halal RWA
- **TrustMark:** Emerald circle.

### 6. Global Diversification
- **Accent:** Sky blue (#0284c7 sky-600). Universal, neutral,
  international.
- **Hero motif:** Faint world-map dot grid, or 6 small region
  markers around the card.
- **Philosophy line:** "Geographic diversification across all
  regions. Maximum spread."
- **Allocation pills:** USDC · cUSD · BRZ · IDRT · Multi-region
- **TrustMark:** Sky circle.

### 7. Custom
- **Accent:** Violet (#7c3aed violet-600). User-defined, personal.
- **Hero motif:** A blank composition slot suggesting "your plan
  goes here" — maybe a dashed-outline placeholder shape, or a
  subtle slider/dial visualization.
- **Philosophy line:** "Set your own allocation. Your plan, your
  proof."
- **Allocation pills:** Configurable · Your targets · Your regions
- **TrustMark:** Violet circle.

## What to avoid

- **Literal religious or cultural imagery.** No literal Adinkra
  symbols, no actual calligraphy characters, no religious imagery.
  Abstract geometry inspired by the tradition is right; literal
  symbol use risks appropriation and looks tacky.
- **Stock photography or flag iconography.** This is a typographic +
  geometric system, not a clipart deck.
- **Identical compositions across cards.** The same anti-pattern
  that the SportWarren rebuild rejected. Each Protection Plan must
  have a distinct hero composition.
- **Crypto-coded visual register.** No charts, no candlesticks, no
  "to the moon" energy. This is a calm savings product.
