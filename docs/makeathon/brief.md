# Config Makeathon — DiversiFi submission brief

**Deadline:** 2026-06-18, 11:59pm PDT (TODAY).
**Target prize:** Building with Purpose ($10k). Not chasing Grand Prize.

## Why DiversiFi fits Building with Purpose

The prize description: *"Most exemplary use of design to solve real
problems for real people."*

DiversiFi is a savings-protection product for **people in volatile
economies** — emerging-market users facing >10% local inflation who
want to protect purchasing power without becoming DeFi traders. The
7 Protection Plans (Africapitalism, Buen Vivir, Confucian, Gotong
Royong, Islamic Finance, Global Diversification, Custom) are
culturally-aligned investment philosophies — not generic "crypto
yields." That cultural framing is genuinely under-explored in
fintech UX.

The submission angle: **"Cultural design language for emerging-market
finance."** Most fintech speaks one visual register (neutral,
Western, terminal-coded). DiversiFi's Protection Plans deserve their
own visual identity each — and the Figma component family makes
that opinion legible.

- Re-architecting the codebase.
- Bringing the entire app's UI into Figma. We're shipping a card
  family + one in-context mockup — that's it.
- Multi-track positioning. This is a *Building with Purpose*
  submission only; don't dilute the narrative chasing other prizes.
